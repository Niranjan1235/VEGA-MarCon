Benefits & Perks

We invest in our people

Health Insurance

Comprehensive group health insurance covering you and your family — hospitalisation, day-care, and OPD expenses covered.

Performance Bonuses

Quarterly and annual incentives tied directly to impact. High performers are recognised and rewarded meaningfully.

Learning Budget

₹30,000/year per employee for courses, certifications, conferences, and books of your choice. No approval gatekeeping.

Mentorship Programme

Every new joiner is paired with a senior leader for 6 months. You get structured guidance, not just a buddy.

Flexible Work Options

Hybrid or remote options available for eligible roles. We measure output, not hours on seat.

Cross-Division Exposure

Opportunity to rotate across Real Estate, Interiors, Construction, and Digital — build a career, not just a job.
