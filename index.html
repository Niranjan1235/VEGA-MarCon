FAQ

Questions
we hear often

Everything you need to know before you begin. Can't find your answer here? Call us or chat — we respond fast.

What cities does Vegamarcon operate in?

+

We currently operate across Bengaluru (head office), Chennai, and Pune. Our flat sales portfolio covers 200+ active listings across all three cities, and our interior design and construction teams are available in all locations. We are expanding to Hyderabad and Mumbai — reach out to be notified.

How much does interior design cost?

+

Interior design costs depend on scope, size, and material choices. For a standard 2BHK in Bengaluru, our packages start at ₹5.5 lakhs for a modular package and go up to ₹18+ lakhs for full premium turnkey. We provide a detailed, itemised quote after a free consultation — no surprises, no hidden charges.

How long does interior design take?

+

A typical 2–3BHK interior project takes 45–70 working days from design sign-off to handover. Larger or custom projects may take longer. We share a detailed milestone schedule upfront and send weekly site progress reports so you're always informed.

Is construction a new service? What should I expect?

+

Yes, construction is our newest offering — launched with the same commitment to precision that defines our interior and sales work. We use licensed structural engineers on every site, Grade A materials, and offer a fixed-price contract so there are zero cost overruns. We currently take on residential villas, apartments, and small commercial builds.

Do you help with home loan assistance?

+

Absolutely. Our in-house finance team works with leading banks and NBFCs to help you find the most competitive home loan rates. We assist with documentation, eligibility checks, and follow-up — completely free of charge as part of our flat sales service.

Are all your properties RERA registered?

+

Yes. Every property in our portfolio is RERA registered and we handle full title verification and legal due diligence in-house before any listing goes live. Our legal advisors accompany you through registration and you receive complete documentation at handover.

What warranty do you offer after handover?

+

Interior design projects come with a 1-year comprehensive defect warranty covering workmanship and materials. Construction projects carry a 1-year structural defect warranty. Beyond warranty, our dedicated after-sales team remains available for maintenance and support — this is a promise, not a policy.
